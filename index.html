Our Product

Captura

3d Capture for the real world

Capture

Captura is our field-deployable volumetric capture solution for converting real world objects and environments into perceptually pleasing 3d models.  Our software combines realtime data streams from a variety of depth sensing and imaging technologies with highly responsive, sub-millimeter accurate tracking for superior results.

advanced toolchain & post processing

Clean

Our post processing tools provide powerful localization, annotation, measurement capabilities. Quickly choose from presets to process captures into textured meshes or use expert features and manipulate clouds with our tools from within a 1st person WebVR experience.

Access Management

Control

Once volumetric assets have been processed, you can share them and authorize their use over our RESTful Service APIs. Asset publishing handles the processing, segmentation and delivery to access controlled content distribution network, assuring that your data is always available to authorized consumers, like your design software or other virtual and augmented reality experiences. You get to control which applications have access to your data.
